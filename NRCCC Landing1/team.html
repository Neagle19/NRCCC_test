The Steering Committee


The National Religious Coalition on Creation Care (NRCCC) is governed by a Steering Committee comprised from a spectrum of Jewish and Christian (Orthodox, Catholic, Protestant, Evangelical) faith communities. The Steering Committee provides counsel to the Executive Coordinator, Fred Kruger.
